Quadratic Expression: What and Why?

A quadratic expression is second order polynomial:

  • \( A x^2 + B x + C \), where \( A, B, C \) are the coefficients, and \( x \) is the independent variable.

  • It is the simplest non-linear expression that is frequently useful in modeling data.

Example

A  <- 2
B  <- 4
C  <- 6
x <- seq(1, 10, by = 1)
y <- A*x^2 + B*x + C
print(data.frame(x = x, y = y))
